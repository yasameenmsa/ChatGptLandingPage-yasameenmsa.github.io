Introducing ChatGPT

We’ve trained a model called ChatGPT which interacts in a conversational way. The dialogue format makes it possible for ChatGPT to answer followup questions, admit its mistakes, challenge incorrect premises, and reject inappropriate requests.

Features

  1. Conversational Interaction: ChatGPT is designed to engage in natural language conversations with users. It can understand and respond to a wide range of queries, making it feel more like interacting with a human rather than receiving predefined responses.
  2. Follow-up Questions and Context Awareness: One of the strengths of ChatGPT is its ability to maintain context during conversations. It can understand follow-up questions, remember information shared earlier in the conversation, and provide relevant responses based on the ongoing context.
  3. Error Correction and Admitting Mistakes: ChatGPT has been trained to recognize when it may not have the correct answer or when it's unsure about a response. It can admit its mistakes or lack of knowledge, which helps build trust with users and ensures more accurate information is provided.
